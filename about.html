Our Mission

At Giggo, we believe flexible digital work should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to empower global users by offering legitimate, easy-to-start microtasks that reward effort, accuracy, and consistency.

Platform Overview

Giggo is a global microtask and reward platform designed for the digital era. Founded in 2018, we’ve grown to support users across 60+ countries with multilingual interfaces, mobile-first workflows, and fast local payouts. Our task ecosystem includes product testing, data labeling, feedback entry, content audit, and more—partnered with trusted enterprise clients.

Our Team

Our leadership brings together decades of experience in crowdsourcing, platform safety, and scalable workforce technology. We work closely with compliance officers and global partners to ensure tasks are fair, secure, and well-compensated.

Compliance & Security

Giggo complies with ISO/IEC 27001 for information security management and adheres to digital labor regulations across Southeast Asia, Latin America, and MENA regions. All task providers undergo vetting, and our reward system includes audit logs, fraud prevention, and multi-layered dispute resolution protocols.
