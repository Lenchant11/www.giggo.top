Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Giggo User Task Hive (“Giggo”, “we”, “our”, or “the Platform”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website or services.

1. What Information We Collect

1.1 Voluntarily Provided: When you submit a contact form, email us, or interact via embedded forms, you may provide us with information such as name, email, or message content.

1.2 Automatically Collected: We collect anonymous usage data including your IP address, browser type, visited pages, time spent, and click behavior through analytics and tracking tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Meta Pixel).

2. Third-Party Tracking Technologies

Our site uses Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, and potentially other third-party SDKs to understand user behavior, improve site performance, and support remarketing or conversion tracking. These services may use cookies, device identifiers, or browser fingerprinting to collect data. You may opt out of these technologies via your browser settings or through third-party opt-out tools (see Section 7).

3. Use of Collected Data

  • To improve site performance, navigation, and user experience
  • To track conversion metrics and campaign performance
  • To deliver personalized advertisements and remarketing content (where applicable)
  • To respond to user inquiries and ensure platform security

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Limited anonymous data may be shared with third-party services such as Google Ads or Meta Ads strictly for analytics or advertising optimization. These partners are bound by their own privacy and compliance standards.

5. Cookies & Consent

We use essential cookies and optional analytics/marketing cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser or choose to reject non-essential cookies where consent banners are available. Disabling cookies may limit functionality.We do not use third-party cookies for tracking purposes, nor do we engage in any form of remarketing activities.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data. While no system is 100% secure, we make ongoing efforts to improve our security posture.

7. Your Rights & Opt-Out Options

  • You may request to view, modify, or delete any personal data you voluntarily provided
  • You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking via this browser add-on
  • You can adjust Meta ad personalization settings at Facebook Ad Preferences
  • To make a data request, email us at privacy@Giggo.work

8. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or tools. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or practices. Please consult those sites directly.

9. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be revised at any time. All changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the website after changes implies your consent.
