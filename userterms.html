User Guide

This user manual helps new users navigate and use the Giggo User Task Hive platform effectively.

1. Getting Started with Giggo

  • Visit the Task Hub to browse online tasks.
  • Each task includes a reward amount, deadline, and clear instructions.
  • Start with beginner tasks to build your reputation and unlock more options.

2. Participating in Online Tasks

  • Go to the Task Hub.
  • Select a task that matches your skill or interest. Common types include surveys, app reviews, short videos, and data entry.
  • Some tasks may require temporary payment for product purchase or identity verification. These are fully reimbursed once verified.
  • Follow the instructions carefully and submit proof via the listed contact method.

3. Rewards and Withdrawals

  • All tasks grant points as rewards. Points are visible in your user dashboard or sent to your registered email.
  • You can convert your accumulated points to cash by contacting support.
  • Withdrawal methods include PayPal, local bank transfer, and mobile wallets depending on your country.
  • To initiate a withdrawal, email support@Giggo.top or message us via Telegram.

4. General Rules and Guidelines

  • Read the full instructions of each task or job before participating.
  • Do not submit fraudulent or falsified information; violations may result in account suspension.
  • Meet deadlines and communicate respectfully with employers and support staff.

5. Support and Dispute Resolution

For help, disputes, or clarifications, reach our support team via:
Email: support@Giggo.top
Telegram: @Giggo1688

6. Safety & Disclaimer

We strive to verify all tasks and employers, but Giggo cannot guarantee the availability or profitability of any opportunity. Be cautious with any tasks involving prepayment—always confirm with official support before proceeding. All rewards are subject to review and approval.
